The Story Behind InfinitiLogicSolutions

InfinitiLogicSolutions is a one-person operation born out of a simple idea: what if I used my weekends to build useful tools instead of just consuming content?

With the rise of AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude, I found myself able to prototype and build things faster than ever before. What used to take months can now be done in a weekend.

This is not a startup or a business plan. It is a hobby that produces real tools. Every project here was built on Saturday mornings with coffee, curiosity, and AI assistance.
